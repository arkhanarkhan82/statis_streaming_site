StreamEast leverages a robust Content Delivery Network (CDN) to distribute data across a global array of servers. This decentralized approach ensures that speed remains optimal regardless of the user's geographic location, effectively minimizing latency. To further enhance connectivity, StreamEast employs Geosynchronous Routing, a technique that automatically connects the user to the nearest and least congested server.
