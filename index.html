Yes. Sportsurge is a completely free aggregator. We believe sports should be accessible to everyone without expensive monthly fees.

How does Sportsurge work?

Think of us as a search engine for sports. We do not host content. Instead, we find and index the best available broadcast links from around the internet and organize them into an easy-to-read schedule.

Can I watch on mobile?

Absolutely. Sportsurge is optimized for all devices, including iPhones, Android smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
