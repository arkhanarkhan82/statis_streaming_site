Prioritizing Speed with Content Delivery Networks

StreamEast leverages a robust Content Delivery Network (CDN) to distribute data across a global array of servers. This decentralized approach ensures that speed remains optimal regardless of the user's geographic location, effectively minimizing latency. To further enhance connectivity, StreamEast employs Geosynchronous Routing, a technique that automatically connects the user to the nearest and least congested server. This ensures that a viewer in Tokyo and a viewer in New York both experience the same high-quality stream, regardless of where the original broadcast signal originates.

Adaptive Bitrate Streaming allows StreamEast to dynamically adjust video quality in real-time.
