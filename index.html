StreamEast leverages a robust Content Delivery Network (CDN) to distribute data across a global array of servers. To further enhance connectivity, StreamEast employs Geosynchronous Routing, a technique that automatically connects the user to the nearest and least congested server. StreamEast adopts the modern HEVC (H.265) Codec for superior visual performance.
